Team

Founders, advisors, and the expert group.

ADPS brings together researchers and practitioners working across agent architecture, software engineering, open-source infrastructure, and enterprise deployment. The team maintains the pattern catalog, publishes reference material, and tests the language against runnable systems and field cases.

Founders

AI researcher at A*STAR Singapore and author of Manning's Designing AI Agents. His work focuses on applying software-engineering discipline to production agent systems: explicit responsibilities, execution topologies, verification boundaries, and reusable patterns. Selected work:

Haofen Wang (王昊奋)

KNOWLEDGE GRAPH · KNOWLEDGE-ENHANCED LLM · SYSTEMATIZATION

Tenured Professor and doctoral supervisor at Tongji University, focused on knowledge graphs and knowledge-enhanced large language models. His core open-source work includes OpenSPG (an enterprise knowledge graph), KAG-Thinker (the first open-source knowledge-enhanced reasoning model, 10k+ GitHub stars), MemOS (a memory-native operating system for LLMs, 1M+ calls/day), and AI-Ceping (an LLM-evaluation crowdsourcing platform, 300k monthly active users) — deployed across airport operations, financial risk control, and in-cabin interaction.

Founding rotating chair of OpenKG, the largest open Chinese knowledge-graph community; executive editor of Data Intelligence; secretary-general of the CCF NLP Technical Committee; deputy secretary-general of the CIPS Language and Knowledge Computing Committee.
